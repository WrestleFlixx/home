About WrestleFlixx

Our Mission

WrestleFlixx was built for wrestling fans who want more than what the big promotions offer. Independent wrestling is where the passion lives — where athletes compete for the love of the sport, and where the most innovative matches happen.

We built WrestleFlixx to give independent wrestling the platform it deserves. Stream full events, catch the matches you missed, and discover new promotions — all in one place, completely free.

Whether you're a lifelong fan or just discovering the world of independent wrestling, WrestleFlixx has something for you.

  • Multiple Promotions — Events from a growing roster of independent wrestling organizations.
  • Free Access — No subscriptions, no paywalls. Wrestling for everyone.
  • Regular Updates — New events added as they happen.
